# Substack notes

Substack is the *notification hub and narrative center* of the overall stack. Notifications for all new repo overviews and guides, and accompanying YouTube videos, are published here.

## Tags/sections/lanes
  - Welcome to the bench--when a new reel comes to the bench, a bit about why and how it was acquired and what the plans are for it. **Tag = New Arrival**

  - At the bench (maybe just change to "Production Notes" or "Lights, camera, action")--production notes on how the images and videos are made. **Tag = Production Notes**

  - New publications--when a new overview + guide + video stack is completely published, a notification. **Tag = New Publication**

  - Reel stories--when service is completed on a reel, a story about taking it into the field, how it performs, where it is integrated with other tackle to make a balanced outfit, what fish are caught. **Tag = Field Stories**

The hero post is tagged "Field Stories."
