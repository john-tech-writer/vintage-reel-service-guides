## Disassembly and reassembly photos

Taken while actually working on reel in tray. At least one image per user action/step. For some steps two or three images to show progression of more intricate moves.

Hands are always in frame. Tools are always shown in use. Maintain a clean workspace. Only show tools actually in use on a specific reel. Keep parts neatly organized in tray so all are clearly visible.

## Cleaning and lubrication photos

Cleaning and lubricating are covered in a generic document, but if there are any reel-specific cleaning/lubricating procedures for a specific reel, photograph those.
