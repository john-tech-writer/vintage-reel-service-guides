# Pflueger Nobby model 1963 overview

*Cover the duty rating, reel type, series or place in maker's lineup, something about the overall lineup-context.*

The Pflueger Nobby is a light to medium duty baitcasting reel,

 part of Penn's 700 Spinfisher series, which ranged from the smallest--the 720, mostly intended for freshwater fishing--to the largest, the mighty 704, designed for heavy saltwater duty. The line capacities, materials, and overall heft of the reels in the 700 series varied widely.

The 720's housing and rotor cup are made of steel, as is the main gear; the rest of the gears are mostly brass. The crosswind block and drag cap are made of white plastic. The spool is made of aluminum.
